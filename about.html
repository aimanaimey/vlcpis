ABOUT VLCPIS 2024

TVLCPIS is a virtual seminar organized by the Language Academy, Faculty of Social Sciences and Humanities, Universiti Teknologi Malaysia. The goal of this seminar is to promote lifelong learning by bringing together bright minds to exchange, learn and discuss a wide range of topics. Upon review, all extended abstracts will be published in the VLCPIS 2024 proceedings with ISBN. MyCite & ERA Publication opportunities available!

OBJECTIVES OF VLCPIS

  • To provide opportunities for students to move effectively in the academic world.
  • To contribute to the increase of search knowledge in the area of language and communication research.
  • Postgraduate students can present and receive feedback on the writings they have produced. The results of student-supervisor collaboration can enhance the publication output of lecturers and faculties through the publication of proceedings, articles in selected journals, and edited books.
  • To provide a platform for researchers in the field of Language and Communication to share research findings and opportunities for researchers to exchange ideas, experiences, and resources in quality educational research, especially those involving Language and Communication.
