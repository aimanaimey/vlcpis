ABOUT VLCPIS

The VLCPIS 2024 seminar will be open for participation not only to UTM students but also to students in the field of Language and Communication at the international level. However, this year, the seminar will be jointly organized with the Language for Specific Purposes International Seminar 2024 (LSP 2024).

Objectives:

· The organization of this seminar is to provide opportunities for students to move effectively in the academic world.

· To contribute to the expansion of knowledge in the field of Language and Communication research.

· Postgraduate students can present and receive feedback on their writings.

· To provide a platform for researchers in the field of Language and Communication to share research findings and opportunities for researchers to exchange ideas, experiences, and resources in quality educational research, especially those involving Language and Communication.
